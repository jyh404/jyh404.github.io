Jonathan Huang

Hello! I'm a MEng student at MIT supervised by Gregory Wornell, currently thinking about matrix quantization. Previously, I did my undergraduate degrees (class of 2025) in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (6-2) and Biology (7), also at MIT.

During my undergrad, I worked with Connor Coley and Jing-Ke Weng.

MIT

While at MIT, I enjoyed helping out with Next House (my undergraduate dorm), Next Haunt (the haunted house escape room we construct every year), MIT Science Bowl, and MIT Quiz Bowl. I also held a variety of separate events, including two Next House Thanksgiving dinners, a marathon relay that still lost to Eliud Kipchoge, and a dorm-wide crossword competition. I really encourage students to hold events with the many resources that MIT offers -- feel free to reach out if you have questions!
